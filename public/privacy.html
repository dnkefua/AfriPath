AfriPath Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

AfriPath is an opportunity discovery and application-readiness app by NDN Analytics. This private testing build helps users review scholarships, curated international jobs, volunteer placements, visa pathways, saved opportunities, application tasks, and readiness checklists.

Information Stored On Your Device

AfriPath stores app preferences, saved opportunities, tracked applications, readiness checklist selections, curation leads, and export snapshots in local app/browser storage on your device. This information is used to keep your app experience available between sessions.

Information Sent To A Server

The private testing build does not require account registration or payment. AfriPath includes an optional backend sync workflow for developer testing. If that workflow is enabled by the developer, a signed-off snapshot of app data may be sent to an AfriPath backend endpoint for app operations, debugging, and content curation review.

External Links

AfriPath links to official opportunity, sponsor, visa, scholarship, and volunteer pages. External sites are controlled by their own operators and have their own privacy practices.

Accounts And Deletion

This closed testing build does not create AfriPath user accounts. You can clear locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app storage from Android settings. If account features are added later, AfriPath will provide account deletion controls and update this policy.

Children

AfriPath is intended for applicants, students, professionals, researchers, and adult volunteers. It is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact NDN Analytics through www.ndnanalytics.com.
