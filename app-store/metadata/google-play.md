## Full Description

AfriPath is a global opportunity intelligence platform for applicants preparing scholarships, international hiring pathways, volunteer placements, sponsor-backed roles, and visa-supported mobility routes.

The app helps users:

- Search verified global opportunities
- Review source-backed eligibility signals
- Save and track opportunities
- Prepare reusable application credentials
- Prioritize next actions through decision intelligence
- Manage applications in a command center
- Capture and publish researched opportunities through a curation workflow

AfriPath is designed for private testing with a focus on opportunity discovery, readiness planning, and application organization.

## Data Safety Draft

This private testing build stores user activity locally in app/browser storage, including saved opportunities, tracked applications, credential readiness toggles, curation leads, and backend export snapshots. The app does not include payments, user-to-user messaging, or production account login in this build.
