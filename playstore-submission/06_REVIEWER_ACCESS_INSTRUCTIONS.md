# Reviewer Access Instructions

## Access

No login is required.

## How To Review

1. Install AfriPath from the closed testing track.
2. Open the app.
3. Start on the Home screen and confirm the app loads without credentials.
4. Use Search to filter scholarships, curated international jobs, volunteer placements, and other opportunities.
5. Open an opportunity detail page and review source links and international applicant eligibility notes. "Apply on Official Site" opens the provider's external page; "Track" saves a private on-device checklist only.
6. Open Visa Hub and review sponsored jobs, visa-supported routes, and sponsor information.
7. Open Command Center and test application tasks, documents, readiness, and curation workflows.
8. Save at least one opportunity and confirm it appears in Saved.
9. Open the Privacy Policy and Support links from the app footer.

## Test Account

Not applicable. AfriPath closed testing build does not require an account, password, OTP, paid access, or location-specific access.
